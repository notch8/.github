### We help institutions build digital repositories scholars actually use

Since 2007, Notch8 has partnered with libraries, universities, and cultural organizations to **design, ship, and sustain** repository software at real-world scale.

- **Repository & research-data platforms** — **Samvera** / **Hyku** & **Hyrax**, **Dataverse**, **DSpace**, **Invenio RDM**, and the integrations they need in production
- **Discovery & publishing** — **Blacklight**, **Spotlight**, **IIIF**, **Manifold**, and Solr-backed discovery where it fits
- **Scale** — Collections from thousands to **millions of objects** and **hundreds of terabytes**
- **Access** — Metadata and interfaces that make cultural and scholarly material usable, not just stored

We work across the ecosystems institutions already run — **Samvera, Dataverse, DSpace, Invenio RDM, Manifold**, and the discovery layers on top.

### Our approach

*After you work with Notch8, your repository is built to last.*

We don’t only deliver software — we stay engaged with the operational reality of digital collections: metadata, integrations, performance, upgrades, and the expectations of scholars and the public. Our team has been at this since **2007**, and we still support institutions we started with.

[![Work With Us](https://img.shields.io/badge/Work_With_Us-E2732F?style=for-the-badge&logoColor=white)](https://www.notch8.com)

### What we build

*Repositories and publishing systems that researchers and the public actually use.*

- **Hyku / Hyrax (Samvera)** — Institutional repositories and multi-tenant collections
- **Dataverse** — Research data repositories, metadata, and deposit workflows
- **DSpace** — Institutional repositories, including current DSpace 7+ deployments
- **Invenio RDM** — Research data repository patterns (Zenodo lineage)
- **Manifold** — Scholarly editions and press-style digital publication
- **Blacklight & Spotlight** — Discovery and curated exhibits on top of strong metadata
- **IIIF** — Interoperable image and object delivery for cultural heritage
- **Fedora & Solr** — Back-end patterns for durable, searchable collections where the stack calls for them
- **Custom development** — Features, migrations, and glue code your institution’s roadmap requires

### Our services

*We meet institutions where they are — and help them get where they need to go.*

- Repository design, implementation, and hardening
- Migration and metadata transformation
- Performance tuning for large collections
- Hosting, maintenance, and ongoing support
- Leadership and contributions across the **open source** communities these stacks depend on
